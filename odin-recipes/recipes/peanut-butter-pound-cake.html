Peanut Butter Pound Cake

Nutty pound cake with a crunchy topping.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups butter, softened
  • 6 eggs
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped peanuts (Optional)
